  • Greta Thunberg has been the spark that initiated this movement or atleast brought the issue to light to so many people this remarkable girl has been fighting climate change since the age of 15 when she began spending her school days outside of Sweedish parliment to call for stronger action to be taken. It is estimated that there is 1.5 acres of forests cut down every second worldwide most of which coming from the Amszon.
