How can I help SEAD?



Here at SEAD we believe everyone can help contribute to saving the planet. This can be done in many different ways that you can easily get involved in. We can easily do this as long as we all work together to help get this done. It does not matter if you are young or old, poor or rich you can still help contribute easily without any extra effort other thana little more thought for the environment

But how can I get involved?

You can get involved easily by:

  • Attend strikes with us
  • Buy local goods
  • Donating
  • Getting involved in your community to set up community compost schemes
  • Planting trees
  • Recycle goods
  • Stop using one use plastics
  • Tell your friends/family about the issue
  • Turn off the lights or any electronics when they are not being used
  • Use public transport or cycle
  • Use your vote to elect politicions that will protect the environment
